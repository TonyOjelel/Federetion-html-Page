Historical Background and Mission

01

Section 1

Founding and Evolution

01

Establishment of FETU

The Federation of Engineers, Technologists and Technicians of Uganda (FETU) was founded in response to the growing need for professional representation and development within the engineering and technology sectors. Since its inception, FETU has evolved to become a leading advocate for the advancement of engineering and technological practices in Uganda.

02

Mission and Vision

FETU's mission is to promote excellence, innovation, and ethical practices in engineering, technology, and related professions. With a vision of fostering sustainable development and societal impact, FTTU aims to elevate the standards and contributions of professionals in the field.

Advocacy and Impact

01

Policy Advocacy

FETU has been instrumental in advocating for policies that support the growth and regulation of engineering and technological practices in Uganda. Through strategic partnerships and engagements, FETU has influenced key decisions and initiatives that shape the industry landscape.

02

Professional Development

The federation is committed to enhancing the professional development of its members through training, mentorship, and knowledge-sharing platforms. By equipping professionals with the latest skills and insights, FETU contributes to the overall advancement of the sector.

03

Community Engagement

FETU actively engages with local communities to promote the importance of engineering and technology in driving sustainable development. This outreach fosters a deeper understanding of the impact of these professions on societal progress.

Milestones and Recognition

01

Industry Milestones

FETU has achieved significant milestones in fostering collaboration among engineers, technologists, and technicians, leading to the implementation of impactful projects and initiatives across various sectors. These milestones underscore the federation's commitment to driving positive change.

02

National Recognition

FETU's contributions to the engineering and technology landscape have been recognized at the national level, positioning the federation as a key influencer in shaping policies and practices that impact the profession and the broader community.

03

International Partnerships

FETU has established partnerships with international organizations and institutions, enabling knowledge exchange, capacity building, and the adoption of global best practices within the Ugandan engineering and technology ecosystem.

Commitment to Diversity and Inclusion

01

Gender and Youth Inclusion

FETU is dedicated to promoting gender equality and youth participation in engineering, technology, and technical fields. Through targeted programs and initiatives, the federation seeks to create a more inclusive and diverse professional landscape.

02

Ethical Standards and Integrity

Upholding ethical standards and integrity is a core principle of FETU, ensuring that professionals within the federation adhere to the highest ethical practices and contribute to the betterment of society through their work.

03

Sustainability and Innovation

FETU is committed to fostering sustainable and innovative practices within the engineering and technology sectors, aligning with global efforts to address environmental challenges and drive technological advancements for the benefit of all.
